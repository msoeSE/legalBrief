What is an Appellant's Reply Brief?

  • An Appellant files two formal documents with the Court of Appeals. The first document is the Appellant’s Initial Brief, which you have already prepared and filed. The second is the Reply Brief.
  • The Reply Brief addresses the facts and arguments presented in the Respondent’s Response Brief. A Reply Brief is short and focused.
  • The rules governing the form, contents and filing of an Appellant's Reply Brief are found in Wis. Stat. §809.19(1) and (4).
